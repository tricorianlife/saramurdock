Impact beyond the dispensary

Sara's portfolio reflects more than two decades of service, innovation, and leadership across pharmacy, healthcare, and community. Recognised as the 2025 VIC Pharmacist of the Year and National Outstanding Community Pharmacist, Sara has led projects that expand access to care, empower vulnerable patients, and strengthen community connections.

Her work spans from establishing vaccination clinics and pioneering pharmacy services to leading Rotary community initiatives, fundraising for children's health charities, and mentoring the next generation of pharmacists.

Each project in this portfolio represents Sara's belief that healthcare is not confined to the walls of a pharmacy, it belongs in schools, sporting clubs, neighbourhood houses, multicultural groups, and every place where people gather.

This collection showcases her impact in healthcare innovation, community advocacy, speaking and media engagement, awards and recognition, and grassroots partnerships that continue to shape healthier, stronger, and more connected communities.
