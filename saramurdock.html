03  /  The Story
Baghdad

Born into conflict

Sara grew up in Iraq during the war — a childhood shaped by uncertainty, courage, and the sheer will to keep going.

Age 12

Across the mountains

Her family fled, held hostage for two months before being smuggled to safety. They reached Australia as refugees, carrying nothing but hope.

20+ years

A life in pharmacy

From cleaning shelves after school to studying at Monash, opening pharmacies across regional NSW, and leading in industry — service became her purpose.

2025

Pharmacist of the Year

Today she leads a patient-first health hub at Pharmacy 777 Pascoe Vale, and a national push for more women to lead in pharmacy.

Accessible. Dignified. Human.

Dignified
